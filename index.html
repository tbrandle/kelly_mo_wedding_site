Our Story

It all started tailgating for a Broncos game Girl is wearing a sequins headband (are you surprised) and trying to get some drinks for her friends

Boy is watching the game on the big screen Girl’s friend (Rachele) knows boy, boy’s friend (Morgan) knows girl and phone numbers are exchanged.

Girl invites boy to the book signing of a new book on skiing and climate change.

Boy says yes and won’t yet admit he has already fallen in love with the totally cool girl that invites boys to these types of things. Did I mentioned it included free whiskey?

Girl waits impatiently for another two weeks before boy finally asks girl out on a real date. (Two weeks?!?)

Girl and boy stuff faces with tacos, guac, and margaritas and realize the possibilities of such a cool partnership.

Boy and girl commence endless ski dates, camping dates, rafting dates, national park dates, international dates, lazy movie night dates, backpacking dates, beach dates, game night dates, competitive dates (we are not competitive at all), red rocks dates, sushi dates, hot springs dates, house buying dates, and garden planting dates.

Boy and girl plan trip down to Telluride Bluegrass Festival.

Boy mistakenly raves to girl how amazing Town Park Camping will be.

Girl realizes there has been a mistake and boy and girl are camping four miles outside of town.

Boy quickly rethinks marrying this high maintenance girl and postpones proposal for next day.

Boy comes to his senses and invites girl on a hike up to Bear Creek Falls.

Boy drops down on one knee and asks girl to share many more dates with boy as his wife.

Girl says yes.

The adventure continues...

When and Where

Ceremony and Reception- August 25, 2018 at Perry Mansfield, Steamboat CO Perry Mansfield quickly captured our hearts. With not one but two stunning aspen groves, rustic cabins, sprawling meadows and the privacy of having the entire property to ourselves, Perry Mansfield is perfect for our mountain souls. We love this unique location and can’t think of a better place to exchange vows than in the middle of a golden aspen grove and dance the night away in the picturesque pavilion.
